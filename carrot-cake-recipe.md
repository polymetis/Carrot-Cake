#Carrot Cake

##Ingredients 

###Basic Cake Mix

*1 cup Sugar
*1 cup Butter
*4 eggs
*1/2 tsp salt
*1 1/3 cup flour
*1 1/3 tsp Baking Powder
*1 1/3 tsp Baking Soda 

###Carrot Cake specifics 

*2 cups of grated carrots
*2 tsp cinnamon 

###Instructions

Mix the basic cake recipe together and then fold carrots into the batter.
